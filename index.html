Book Overview

Molly Faris is a raw and unfiltered true-life novel chronicling a daughter's return to her Midwestern roots to bid farewell to her terminally ill father. Through Molly's witty and unrefined lens, the narrative explores familial conflicts, hidden guilt, and the profound impact of impending mortality.

About The Author

Molly Faris

Molly Faris emerges as a distinctive voice in contemporary literature, known for her unfiltered storytelling and fearless exploration of complex human experiences. Her writing in "Route 47" demonstrates a rare combination of humor, rawness, and spirituality, creating a narrative that is both captivating and thought-provoking.

Faris's ability to navigate the delicate nuances of familial relationships, coupled with her unapologetic approach to tackling taboo subjects, sets her apart as a writer unafraid to push boundaries. The emotional depth she brings to her characters and the exploration of life's profound questions showcase a keen understanding of the human condition.
